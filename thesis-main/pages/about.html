About Up Cycle

Revolutionizing waste management through AI

About Us

        UpCycle is a application designed to promote sustainable waste management by leveraging machine learning technology. The app allows users to simply scan or upload an image of a waste item, and it intelligently classifies it as biodegradable or non-biodegradable. UpCycle aims to make waste segregation easy, accurate, and accessible for everyone—encouraging responsible disposal habits and contributing to a cleaner environment. With user-friendly features, UpCycle is your smart companion toward a more sustainable future.

Our Mission

        To empower individuals and communities with smart, eco-friendly technology that simplifies waste segregation using machine learning — promoting sustainable habits and reducing environmental impact, one item at a time.

What We Do

Using advanced technology and intuitive design, UpCycle makes waste sorting effortless.

  • Classify with Confidence: Upload waste images, and our classifier instantly identifies whether it's biodegradable or non-biodegradable.
  • Educate and Inform: Learn about proper waste disposal techniques and how you can contribute to environmental preservation.
  • Empower Communities: Together, we can reduce landfill waste and improve recycling efficiency.

Whether you're an individual, a business, or part of a larger organization, UpCycle is here to inspire a more sustainable lifestyle. Join us on our journey toward a cleaner planet!
